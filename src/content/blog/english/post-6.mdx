## Best Gifts for Energetic 3-Year-Olds

Three-year-olds are curious, energetic, and endlessly creative. These gifts channel all that energy into fun and learning.

### Active Play

- **Balance Bike** — Build confidence on two wheels before transitioning to pedals
- **Indoor Climbing Triangle** — Safe, sturdy, and great for rainy days
- **Trampoline with Handle** — Burn energy without leaving the living room

### Creative Play

- **Play-Doh Creativity Kit** — Hours of squishing, shaping, and imagining
- **Magnetic Tile Set** — Build towers, castles, and anything they dream up
- **Washable Marker Art Set** — Let them create without the mess stress

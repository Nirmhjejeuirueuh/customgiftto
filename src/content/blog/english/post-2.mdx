## The Best Gifts for 1-Year-Olds

Shopping for a one-year-old can be tricky. They're at that magical stage where everything is new and exciting. Here are expert-approved picks that encourage development while keeping things fun.

### Developmental Toys

- **Stacking Rings** — Helps with hand-eye coordination and color recognition
- **Shape Sorter** — Teaches problem-solving skills and shape identification
- **Soft Building Blocks** — Safe for little hands and great for creative play

### Books for Early Readers

- **Touch-and-Feel Board Books** — Sensory books that engage curious fingers
- **Musical Nursery Rhyme Book** — Introduces rhythm and language through song
- **Personalized Name Book** — A custom storybook featuring their name

## Finding the Perfect Graduation Gift

Graduation season is here, and finding the perfect gift for a high school grad can feel overwhelming. Whether they're heading off to college, taking a gap year, or entering the workforce, we've curated the best gifts that are practical, thoughtful, and memorable.

### Dorm Essentials They'll Actually Use

Starting college means setting up a brand new living space. These gifts help them feel at home from day one:

- **Premium Bedding Set** — A cozy duvet and pillow set goes a long way in a tiny dorm room
- **Compact Mini Fridge** — Perfect for late-night snacks and keeping drinks cold
- **Desk Organizer Kit** — Keep their study space tidy and productive

### Tech That'll Keep Them Connected

Today's students rely on technology for everything from classes to staying in touch with family:

- **Noise-Canceling Headphones** — Essential for studying in noisy dorm environments
- **Portable Charger** — A high-capacity power bank for those long days on campus
- **Smart Speaker** — Set timers, play music, and manage their schedule hands-free

### Keepsakes They'll Treasure

Some gifts are meant to last a lifetime and remind them of where they came from:

- **Custom Photo Book** — Compile their best high school memories in a beautiful hardcover book
- **Engraved Jewelry** — A personalized necklace or bracelet with their graduation date
- **Handwritten Letter Kit** — Give them a set of letters to open throughout their first year
